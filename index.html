Zone System

8 rhythms.
One daily directive.

Your day maps to a color. Each color carries a distinct rhythm identity — a character, a wave pattern, and behavioral guidance specific to that zone. Your calendar shows which zone you're in each day, before the day starts.

Pink
The Sparkplug
Fast ignition energy

The rhythm that starts things. Pink fires from any state — near-zero ramp time, high dopamine, fast momentum. It reads the room before others do and burns bright in short bursts. The window rewards action over analysis. The burst is the nature, not a symptom of it.

Directive
Full ignition. Move fast, trust first reads, size up. The energy itself is the edge. Pattern recognition fires ahead of analysis — the read arrives before you can explain it, and it's usually correct. Run with it. The window doesn't last long at the top. Don't verify your way out of the signal.
PRESSFULL IGNITION
// Energy pattern
Instant ignition · sharp peak · fork at the top. Less time to catch the fork than Purple. Empowered: long taper, can re-ignite if momentum is sustained.
Orange
The Metaphysical Shield
Protective creative energy

Steady, body-level, and safe. Orange doesn't ignite — it builds. The floor is higher here. Even when things don't go as planned, Orange softens the blow and keeps structure intact. No peak to fall from. Worst case is stagnant, not collapse. The safest window in the system for sustained effort.

Directive
Engaged and protected. Build, create, execute. This is a volume day — sustained effort converts here. Don't wait for the surge. The steady state is the signal. Reliable, repeatable execution. The routine runs itself. Orange doesn't ask you to press harder — it asks you to keep going.
PRESSSUSTAINED BUILD
// Energy pattern
Slow continuous climb · no peak · no fall. Stable floor. Execution-heavy, risk-forward profile. Unempowered = stagnant, not crashing.
Blue
The Pursuer
Curiosity in motion

Always rising toward something. Blue is the rhythm of curiosity — it questions, pursues, locks in with intensity, and cycles through distinct phases: rising, landing, resetting. It rewards going deeper, not wider. Cycles may connect across sessions, days, or years.

Directive
Lock one target and move toward it. Blue rewards depth and convergence. The directive shifts with the cycle phase — the calendar tells you where in the arc you are. On the rise: press with full intensity. At the landing: stay steady, don't over-adjust. At the reset: consolidate, refuel. The next rise begins from here.

Yellow
The Phoenix
Extreme energy · system true neutral

No middle register — spikes up or spikes down from a flat baseline. Yellow is the system's true neutral and its most volatile zone. The dampening is off — what you bring into the window expresses without filtering. Polarity is live. There is no coasting in Yellow.

Directive
Check your state before you sit. Yellow amplifies whatever you bring to it. Regulated state: maximum force available. The gut confirms edge before the math, and the math catches up. Unregulated state: same magnitude fires downward. The work happens before the session starts.

Green
The Stabilizer
Balance · connection · quiet influence

Green works on environments and other people more than on the self. It attunes, stabilizes, and transmits coherence outward. High-coherence window — not high voltage. Green reads the room better than any other zone and lets things self-correct rather than forcing resolution.

Directive
Stay present. Don't force moves. Composure over ambition. You hear what people mean, not just what they say. The live tell is more valuable than the technical read today. Relational intelligence is your edge. Let the environment bring information to you.

Purple
The Cautious
Purpose-driven · momentum-dependent

Purple builds slowly and sustains deeply. It needs a running start and resists disruption once moving. The window sits in identity territory — things feel more significant, more personal, more charged. That charge is data, not direction. You're operating on a longer time horizon than others at the table.

Directive
Slower decision-making is calibration, not weakness. Name what you're feeling before acting. Separate the signal from the story. The edge is real — you can see where the session is going before others feel it turning. But the timing trap: you can be right about the arc and early on the hand. Early and wrong have the same P&L. Move deliberately.

The Catalyst

The direction of the eventual detonation is not guaranteed by the direction of the firing. Authority required — without it, Red drains rather than catalyzes.

Directive
Play with authority or don't play. One question before every significant move: is this authorized, or is the energy looking for a release? When authorized: maximum impact available. Authorized Red shifts outcomes. Reactive Red drains. Half-committed action in this window depletes. The professional move requires a verified hand.
