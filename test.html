Pink
The Sparkplug
Fast ignition energy

The rhythm that starts things. Pink fires from any state — near-zero ramp time, high dopamine, fast momentum. It reads the room before others do and burns bright in short bursts. The window rewards action over analysis. The burst is the nature, not a symptom of it.

Directive
Full ignition. Move fast, trust first reads, size up. The energy itself is the edge. Pattern recognition fires ahead of analysis — the read arrives before you can explain it, and it's usually correct. Run with it. The window doesn't last long at the top.

Protective creative energy

Steady, body-level, and safe. Orange doesn't ignite — it builds. The floor is higher here. Even when things don't go as planned, Orange softens the blow and keeps structure intact. No peak to fall from. Worst case is stagnant, not collapse. The safest window in the system for sustained effort.

Directive
Engaged and protected. Build, create, execute. This is a volume day — sustained effort converts here. Don't wait for the surge. The steady state is the signal. Orange doesn't ask you to press harder — it asks you to keep going.

Slow continuous climb · no peak · no fall. Stable floor. Unempowered = stagnant, not crashing.

Curiosity in motion

Always rising toward something. Blue is the rhythm of curiosity — it questions, pursues, locks in with intensity, and cycles through distinct phases: rising, landing, resetting. It rewards going deeper, not wider.

Directive
Lock one target and move toward it. Blue rewards depth and convergence. On the rise: press with full intensity. At the landing: stay steady, don't over-adjust. At the reset: consolidate, refuel. The next rise begins from here.

No middle register — spikes up or spikes down from a flat baseline. Yellow is the system's true neutral and its most volatile zone. The dampening is off — what you bring into the window expresses without filtering. There is no coasting in Yellow.

Directive
Check your state before you sit. Yellow amplifies whatever you bring to it. Regulated state: maximum force available. Unregulated state: same magnitude fires downward. The work happens before the session starts.

Directive
Stay present. Don't force moves. Composure over ambition. The influence is real — it just operates through environment rather than action. The steadier you hold, the more the room moves toward you.

Directive
